About Us

lvlUP Tutoring is a tutoring platform built by DKCS INC. just for JSU students

Our Story

Welcome to our peer tutoring website! We are a group of students from Jackson State University who are passionate about helping our fellow students succeed academically. As part of our senior project, we have created this platform to connect students who need academic support with other students who are knowledgeable in those subjects. Our team consists of dedicated individuals who are committed to making a positive impact on our campus community. We believe that students can learn best from their peers, and we have designed our tutoring service to facilitate this peer-to-peer learning. Our peer tutors are students who have excelled in their coursework and are passionate about sharing their knowledge with others. We are proud to be part of the Jackson State University community and are committed to giving back. Through this project, we hope to promote academic excellence and create a culture of peer support on our campus. Thank you for visiting our website, and we encourage you to take advantage of our peer tutoring service.
