Frequently Asked Questions

What subjects do you offer tutoring for?

We offer tutoring for a variety of subjects, including math, science, English, history, and more. Our tutors are highly qualified and knowledgeable in their respective fields.

How much does tutoring cost?

Tutoring is free for all JSU students. We believe that every student should have access to academic support regardless of their financial situation.

Can I choose my tutor?

Yes, you can choose your tutor based on availability and subject expertise. We encourage you to read tutor bios and reviews to find the best match for your learning style and academic needs.
